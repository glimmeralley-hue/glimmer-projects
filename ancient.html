CHINESE HAND FAN

Originating over 3000 years ago,the fans quickly became a status symbol for chinese nobility

They were traditionally made from silk and feathers,designed and reinforced with bamboo and ivory frames They were canvases for calligraphy,landscape paitings,representing the rich culturakl heritage of the chinese dynasty
